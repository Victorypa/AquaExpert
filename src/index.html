About the company

Our company provides assistance to the manufacturers of bottled drinking water from Russia when entering new market places.

We see great potential in this market segment, because water becomes a product; there are many manufacturers working only on the local market and interested in entering new market places (for export) in Russia, and under the conditions of climate change, human activity and population growth, a number of world regions will increasingly need clean drinking water.

Cooperation

We invite importers to cooperation by offering a product that will become one of the most popular in the world in the near future - bottled drinking water. The origin of water is Russia, which has one of the largest reserves of fresh water in the world. We guarantee water quality and compliance with the sanitary standards.
